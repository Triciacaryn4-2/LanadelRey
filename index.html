Lana Del Rey

Elizabeth Woolridge Grant, known professionaly as Lana Del Rey is an american singer and songwriter. Her music is noted for cinematic quality and exploration for tragic romance, glamour, melancholia with frequent references to pop culture and 1950s to 70s Americana. Her vintage Hollywood glamour aesthetic is presented not only in her music, but also in her music videos.

Born: June 21, 1985 (age 38 years)
